Themes

  • Multi-agents systems, and more specifically adaptive multi-agent systems (AMAS)
  • Distributed optimization by cooperation between agents
  • Resource allocation
  • Self-organisation, as a mechanism to design artificial systems
  • Software engineering, to build such systems
  • Application to collective robotics, space systems, UAVs
